Our Mission

Empowering global farming through Smart Crop Disease Detection.

Our mission is to make crop disease detection simple and accessible — farmers can upload a single leaf image and instantly know what disease is affecting their crop, along with treatment recommendations.

What Our System Offers

The Technology
ML-Based Disease Detection A Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on the PlantVillage dataset detects crop diseases from leaf images with high accuracy.
Crop Coverage
Supports 4 crops — Apple, Corn, Potato, and Strawberry — covering 15 diseases and healthy plant detection.
Treatment Recommendations
Every diagnosis includes the disease name, confidence score, recommended fertilizer, dosage quantity, and step-by-step treatment guide.
